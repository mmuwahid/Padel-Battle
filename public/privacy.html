Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 22, 2026 · Last updated: June 22, 2026

PadelHub ("we", "us", "our") is a padel league management and match tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the PadelHub mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

By using PadelHub, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used for authentication, account recovery, and essential service communications
  • Display name — shown to other players in your leagues
  • Profile photo (optional) — uploaded by you for your player profile
  • Authentication provider data — if you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email from that provider

1.2 Player Profile Data

You may optionally provide:

  • Country / nationality
  • Playing position (left/right)
  • Handedness (left/right)
  • Gender
  • Date of birth
  • Player grade / self-assessment

1.3 Match and League Data

When you participate in leagues, we collect:

  • Match scores and results
  • Season and league membership data
  • Player statistics and rankings (calculated from match data)
  • Tournament and game mode participation
  • Open match votes and preferences

1.4 Subscription and Payment Data

If you subscribe to PadelHub Pro:

  • We receive subscription status, plan type, and billing period from Apple App Store or Google Play Store via RevenueCat (our subscription management provider)
  • We do NOT collect or store your payment card details, bank information, or billing address. All payment processing is handled directly by Apple or Google.

1.5 Technical Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • Crash reports and error logs (for debugging purposes)

1.6 What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise geolocation data
  • We do not access your contacts, camera roll, or microphone (camera access is only used when you choose to upload a profile photo)
  • We do not use tracking technologies for advertising purposes
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties

3.1 With Other Players

Your display name, avatar, country, playing position, grade, and match statistics are visible to other members of leagues you belong to. This is core to how PadelHub works — league members can see each other's profiles and rankings.

3.3 We Do NOT

  • Sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers
  • Share your data with marketing partners
  • Use your data for targeted advertising
  • Share your email address with other users (only your display name is shown)

3.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or government request, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of PadelHub, our users, or the public.

4. Data Retention

  • Active accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts: When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days. Anonymised match statistics may be retained to preserve league history integrity.
  • Subscription data: Billing records are retained for up to 3 years to comply with financial record-keeping requirements.
  • Technical logs: Crash reports and error logs are automatically deleted after 90 days.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate data (you can do this directly in the app via your profile settings).
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data. You can initiate this from Settings within the app, or by contacting us.
  • Data portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.
  • Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interest.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption in transit)
  • Data is stored in Supabase with row-level security (RLS) — users can only access data they are authorised to see
  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (handled by Supabase Auth)
  • We do not store payment credentials
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorised personnel

No system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.

7. Children's Privacy

PadelHub is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. International Data Transfers

PadelHub is operated from the United Arab Emirates. Our service providers (Supabase, Vercel, RevenueCat) are based in the United States. By using PadelHub, you consent to the transfer of your data to these jurisdictions. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Third-Party Links

PadelHub may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of PadelHub after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
