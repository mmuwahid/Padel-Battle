4 · The answer scale (consistency + pressure = the spine)

Every dimension is scored on this same i–v ladder. Consistency & "holds under pressure" are baked in here, so they are not separate dimensions.

i0 — Error-driven; slow pace only; shot avoided
ii1 — Structured but medium-pace only; loses shape when rushed
iii2 — Reliable & consistent at a medium pace
iv3 — Reliable at medium-high pace, under pressure
v4 — Sustains high pace & intensity across a full match; weaponised
